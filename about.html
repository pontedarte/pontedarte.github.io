Invitation

 

We believe that slowing down is the secret to developing artist’s unique sensibility and vision. Artists deserve a place to step out of the contemporary urban cult of speed, to devote quiet time for re-evaluating the quality and meaning of their artistic practice. 

- Prof Kris van ‘t Hof & Celia de Villiers

You are invited to make time for your creativity. Ponte d'Arte is for your quiet retreats and getaways.  It is a place for you to focus on creative projects, art-making or writing. Take long walks in nature or in our small village. Fresh bread delivery daily, and fresh fish twice a week, a 15-minute drive to the supermarkets, banks, and 7 minutes to a smaller grocery store.

We welcome students, lecturers, artists, designers, architects, ecologists, musicians and other creative persons. 

An exceptional meeting place

  • An art gallery space / concert hall
  • Studios
  • Accommodation
  • Meeting facilities
  • The smallest art gallery in Portugal
  • An inspiring cultural setting and rich historical landscape

It is a place where both conceptual development and technical skills in different media share equal importance. 

Accommodate Individuals or Groups 
At Ponte d’ Arte we can accommodate several groups and individuals who want to work independently, or in collaboration with professors.

Host workshops
Stay for a day, week or month.  We can currently accommodate up to 15 people for a variety of workshops or conferences where artists can experience village life in the countryside.

Attend Master Classes 

Twice a year, during March and September/October, we host international contemporary art students for site-specific master classes and workshops, including conceptual development, drawing, painting, sculpture.

Artist Residencies 

For longer term visitors such as writers, artists, performers, poets, ecologists, musicians, or academics on sabbatical, we offer two private 'artist in residence' apartments.  Currently all funding is undertaken by the resident.
