Accommodation

Ponte Da Mucelia is a fairly remote village, 30 km from Coimbra, with a charming historical bridge. The residency consists of a refurbished neoclassical villa (1930-40) and a newly renovated stone farmhouse (1888) built on top of an ancient Roman road.

We currently have two 'artist in residence' apartments, two three-bedroomed houses and four studio-rooms (single or double), arguably the smallest gallery in Portugal (a former gasoline pay-station) and three big terraces with magnificent vistas on the river, bridge and wooded hills.

 

This complex is currently undergoing conversion to become a meeting centre with large and small exhibition spaces, 300m2 gallery/concert hall, video rooms, a conference room, an art library, studios and workshops, and a small local museum.
