Drawing & Painting - 2016

In Situ Workshop, April 2016

Artist mentors:  Prof Kris van ’t Hof & Celia De Villiers

Art Practice Workshop, 18- 30 July 2016

 

Drawing, painting and environmental interventions in the landscape.  Diverse angles with a different objective in mind each day (contrast, rhythm, linear qualities, composition and perspectives).

  • Morning programme (10 till 12 a.m.) drawing/painting outdoors
  • Lunch break (12 a.m. till 2 p.m.)
  • Afternoon programme (2 till 4 p.m.) Figure drawing indoors (with a professional Model and personalities from the village) 2 hours sketching in various classic and experimental ways.
  • Feedback and discussions: (4 till ±6 p.m.)
