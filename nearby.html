Nearby

 

You might come to stay in a forgotten village, but that does not mean there is nothing to se and do.  Apart from the langscape in walking distance, inspiring day trips are warmly recommended. There is is a wide variety of cultural and lifestyle attractions within 30 mins drive of Ponte d'Arte.

The venue is not far from:

  • Conimbriga, a unique Roma archeological site with exquisite mosaics,
  • Moinhos (windmills)
  • and ancient stone-age housing.
